WESTEROS HOLDS MANY SECRETS

Experience Westeros like never before. Explore the seven kingdoms that make up Westeros in the vast open world of Camino Real. Discover vivid landscapes, massive cities, backwater towns, untamed wilderness, and the people that inhabit them.

🏰 Explore Iconic Locations

Roam about iconic regions from the splendor of King's Landing to the frigid unease of Castle Black, located at the foot of the 700-foot tall Wall.

⚔️ Original Journey

Forge your own legacy as an illegitimate child from House Tyre. Restore your house to its former glory while navigating complex power struggles.

🗡️ Dynamic Combat System

Featuring fully manual controls, dodging, parrying, and emphasizing impactful swordfights designed to immerse players in intense battles.

🛡️ Choose Your Class

Three distinct classes inspired by Game of Thrones: Knight, Sellsword, and Assassin. Each with unique strengths and combat mechanics.

👥 Co-op Content

Encounter dangerous beasts and creatures from the Game of Thrones universe. Defeat them alongside other lords to earn generous rewards.

❄️ The Night's Watch

Aid the Night's Watch in preparations for the final confrontation with the White Walkers and their army of the dead beyond the Wall.
